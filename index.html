About

I'm a Research Engineer at IBM Research, where I build training infrastructure for large language models. My work spans pretraining, post-training alignment, and agentic systems.

Recently I contributed to Bamba — a hybrid Mamba2-Attention architecture pretrained on ~2.2T tokens, adopted as IBM's Granite 4 flagship — and led the post-training pipeline for a 70B+ reasoning model, covering 4-round iterative SFT and GRPO-based RL across 8K→32K→64K context phases.

Before that I built an agentic browser pipeline for RL rollout generation across 23 real-world environments — work that directly informs the writing on this log.
